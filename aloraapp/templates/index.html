About Us

ALO PHYSIO CENTRE, under Alora International Pvt. Ltd. (est. 2023), is committed to delivering personalized physiotherapy and mental wellness care, with a special focus on the elderly and individuals with chronic pain. We blend modern physiotherapy techniques with emotional support to promote holistic healing and improved quality of life.

Rooted in our belief that healthcare is not just about treatment but a journey toward better living, we provide a holistic range of services that address physical rehabilitation, mobility restoration, and mental wellness. Our integrated care model is thoughtfully designed to cater to the unique challenges of geriatric health, combining modern physiotherapy techniques with emotional and psychological support.

Restoring Balance: Home-Based Therapy for Body & Mind

At Alo Physio Centre, we provide expert home physiotherapy services designed to bring healing and care directly to your doorstep. Our skilled physiotherapists create personalized treatment plans to help with post-surgery recovery, injury rehabilitation, pain management, and improved mobility. With hands-on therapy and professional guidance, we ensure you regain strength, flexibility, and independence – all within the comfort of your home.

At Alo Physio Centre, we understand that true wellness goes beyond physical health. That’s why we also offer professional home counseling services to support your mental and emotional well-being. Whether you’re facing stress, anxiety, or life’s personal challenges, our experienced counselors provide a safe and compassionate space to guide you toward clarity and peace of mind. Together, we focus on building balance for both body and mind, helping you live a healthier, happier life.
