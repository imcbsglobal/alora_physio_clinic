Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Alora International Pvt. Ltd. is a healthcare-driven company committed to promoting better living through personalized and professional care. Under the brand name ALO PHYSIO CENTRE, we provide specialized services in geriatric-centered pain management, physiotherapy, and mental wellness therapies.

With our first clinic successfully running at T.C. Palya, and the second branch launched at SRI ARCA Diabetes & General Clinic, we continue our mission to serve the community with compassion, expertise, and integrity.
